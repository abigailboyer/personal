Title

Point of Sale Redesign

Timeframe

Problem Statement

The vision of this UX case study is to improve loyalty creation and account lookup on a retailer's point of sale system by reducing friction for users and customers. I began by conducting user research on site and then created a new UX and UI for the POS application.
